About Us

Paryavaran Welfare Society was set up in the year 2009, as a scheme to uphold biodiversity, guard “paryavaran” (environment), and save energy resources at local level.

Our Initiatives

🌳 Plantation Drives

To increase the green cover we carry out extensive plantation activities all year.

♻️ Dustbin Installation

To promote the Swachh Bharat Abhiyan and prevent littering in our city, we have installed dustbins. These dustbins are cleaned on a regular basis.

🩸 Blood Donation

Organised annually, the prime focus is to make sure that our blood banks never run out of the same. During dengue breakout we realised there was a shortage of certain blood groups. So to eradicate this measure PWS carries out blood donation camps. This always receives an overwhelming support from our volunteers.

🚲 Cycle Rallies

To raise voice against the rising air pollution levels, we conduct cycle rallies to promote the use of bicycles and thus focusing on two issues, lowering air pollution and fitness for all.

🪺 Nest Installation

We install artificial nests on tree branches. We even install eating trays and drinking bowls for birds.

🎨 Painting Activities

Painting alongside dividers, poles and graffiti to promote awareness is the most recent measure the students of the university have taken up.

💨 Pollution Camps

We organise pollution checkup camps to regulate the pollution levels caused by diesel vehicles.

🚗 Car Bin

To prevent littering on roads, our society developed car bins that can be easily be put in the cars.

🚚 Free distribution

We distribute free jute bags, CFL bulbs, cycles, to the needy for more use of Eco-friendly materials to cut down on pollution.
